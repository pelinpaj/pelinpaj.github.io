Elin Amanda Johansson

Singer/Songwriter and More

Elin Amanda Johansson is a singer/songwriter and creator. Growing up on the west coast of Sweden she played classical piano since early years. Her musical roots are also in jazz and Swedish folk music. Today she’s primarily a vocalist but plays the piano and the accordion as well. Apart from music, she has always been writing poetry, made films and videos, and she has a degree in literature and journalism. Her lyrics are a mix of brutal realism, spirituality and a dark sense of humor. She now lives in small town Knivsta with her two sons and husband.


Albums


Avgrunden, 2019
Elin Amanda's first album in Swedish, with a mini-sized, analog, electric piano as only accompaniment. The retro futuristic sound goes well with the often science fiction-inspired and dystopian theme. Still, it's playful and ingenious.

Inspired by classic pastoral poetry, this album is a world of beauty and joy, but with a twist. Ola Jansson's piano by Anna Johansson's violin adds a bit of jazz and folk music to the sound.
